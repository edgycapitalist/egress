# SIVB March-2023 Bank Stress

SVB Financial's March 2023 collapse is a useful reference for funding and
confidence stress. In bank-style episodes, price action can be driven by deposit
flight, funding pressure, credit concerns, and suspension or halt risk, not only
by ordinary shareholder crowd psychology.

Calibration notes: for bank-run-like scenarios, the analyst and critic should
avoid claiming that behavioural archetypes alone explain the move. The correct
language is that the simulation captures an exit-liquidity stress mechanism while
funding and balance-sheet channels remain scenario assumptions.
