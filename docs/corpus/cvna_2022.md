# CVNA Late-2022 Distressed Unwind

Carvana traded through a severe balance-sheet and liquidity scare in late 2022.
The useful lesson for Egress is not that every stressed equity should follow the
same path, but that a fragile name can gap lower, lose natural buyers, and leave
large holders facing a very different exit problem from the one implied by normal
average volume.

Calibration notes: a plausible stress simulation for this episode should show a
large drawdown, material unsold quantity when the exit is large relative to buyer
depth, and market-maker support that withdraws as price stress rises. Treat this
as an illustrative historical analogue, not an exact intraday reconstruction.
