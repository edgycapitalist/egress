Under this scenario, the full 15,000,000-share position in SPY was sold (100% filled).  Selling pushed the price from an arrival of 450.00 to 447.20 (max drawdown 1%); the position sold at a VWAP of 447.49. That cost 56 bps of implementation shortfall and 56 bps of slippage versus arrival.  No volatility halt triggered.  Full exit took 162 ticks.  The cascade came from forced and panic sellers overwhelming thin bargain-hunter and market-maker support as the shocks landed: each break in the price armed the next tranche of sellers, draining the book faster than buyers replenished it.
